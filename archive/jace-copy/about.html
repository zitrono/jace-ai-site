Don't just chat. Act.

At Zeta Labs, we are dedicated to the art of possibility and the pursuit of significance. In an era brimming with distractions, our mission is to develop technologies that clear the path for humanity to engage with what truly matters.

Our values

Our principles guide us in building world-class products through speed, simplicity, and continuous improvement. We focus on what matters most: moving fast while maintaining quality.

Speed of Progress
We believe speed is our competitive advantage. It enables us to iterate faster, try more directions, and build better products through rapid improvement cycles.
Reduce Complexity
We optimize for simplicity in our tech stack, team structure, and features. Every addition must prove its worth, as complexity is the enemy of speed.
Fast Iteration Cycles
From CI/CD to PR reviews, we optimize every step of our development process to minimize friction and maximize development velocity.
Leverage Tools
We embrace tools and external services that boost our productivity, moving complexity away from our codebase while maintaining simplicity.
Question Everything
We challenge assumptions, routinely simplify processes, and think meta-level about solutions to ensure we're moving in the right direction.
Care About People
We value high-bandwidth communication, personal connections, and small-group discussions. Our team's wellbeing drives our success.
