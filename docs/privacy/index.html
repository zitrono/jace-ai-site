Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2025

Overview

At Beneficious ("we," "our," or "us"), we take your privacy seriously. Ralph is designed with a unique deployment model that ensures your data never leaves your control. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with Ralph and our services.

Our Privacy-First Architecture

Ralph operates on a fundamentally different model than typical SaaS platforms:

  • Ralph runs entirely on your own infrastructure
  • Your data never leaves your servers or cloud environment
  • We have no access to your deal information, portfolio data, or any proprietary information
  • Think of Ralph as software you install, not a service you send data to

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: First name, last name, email address, phone number, company name, job title
  • Account Information: Username, password, and account preferences
  • Communication Records: Support tickets, feedback, and correspondence
  • Professional Information: Company details and role-specific information for service customization

Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, operating system
  • Website Analytics: Pages visited, time spent, referral sources (marketing website only)
  • Technical Logs: Error reports and system performance data (when shared voluntarily)

What We Absolutely Do NOT Collect

  • Business Data: We never collect, store, or access your deal information, portfolio data, or proprietary business information
  • Usage Analytics from Ralph: No tracking or analytics within the Ralph application itself
  • Training Data: We never use your data to train or improve our AI models
  • Cross-Client Data: We never share or combine data between different client deployments

Security & Compliance

We implement commercially reasonable technical, administrative, and organizational measures to protect your information from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access:

Enterprise Security Architecture

  • Infrastructure Security: Ralph inherits and extends your organization's existing security standards
  • Authentication Integration: Full support for SAML/SSO, identity providers, and multi-factor authentication
  • Monitoring & Compliance: Compatible with your SIEM tools, audit logging, and compliance frameworks
  • Air-Gapped Support: Deployable in completely isolated environments with no external connectivity
  • Data Governance: Respects and enforces your existing data governance policies and procedures

Security Certifications & Standards

  • SOC 2 Compliance: We are actively pursuing SOC 2 Type I and Type II certifications
  • Industry Standards: Built to support various compliance requirements including GDPR, CCPA, and financial regulations
  • Secure Development: Regular code audits, penetration testing, and encrypted communications
  • Third-Party Assessments: Independent security reviews and vulnerability assessments

Data Protection

Important: While we implement robust security measures for any data we handle, remember that with Ralph's architecture, your sensitive business data never leaves your controlled environment. You maintain complete control over:

  • All security configurations and access controls
  • Data encryption, backup, and disaster recovery procedures
  • User permissions and audit trail management
  • Compliance with your industry-specific regulations

LP and Confidential Information

Limited Partner (LP) information and all confidential data are treated with the highest level of security:

  • Access permissions are fully configurable by your team
  • Role-based access controls match your firm's existing permissions
  • Complete audit trails of all agent activities
  • Data segregation between portfolio companies is maintained

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our marketing website to improve your experience:

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality, user authentication, and fraud prevention. These cannot be disabled.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences, login details, and language settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand website traffic and usage patterns (with your consent)
  • Cookie Policy Acceptance: Track whether you've accepted our cookie usage

Cookie Management

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

Important: No tracking cookies or analytics are used within the Ralph application itself - only on this marketing website.

Data Retention

Since Ralph operates on your infrastructure:

  • You control all data retention policies
  • Data deletion is managed according to your organization's policies
  • We retain only minimal contact information for business relationships
  • Support communications are retained only as long as necessary

Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Control Rights

  • Right to Access: Request information about what personal data we have about you
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing where consent is the legal basis
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, commonly used format

Communication Preferences

  • Marketing Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Communication Preferences: Control how and when we contact you

Ralph Application Data

For data within your Ralph deployment, you have complete control as the data controller. You can manage user access, data retention, and deletion according to your organization's policies.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@beneficious.com.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

How We Share Information

We may share your personal information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who provide services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, customer support)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales (with notice to you)
  • With Your Consent: Any other sharing will be done only with your explicit consent

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Contact Information: Retained while you have an active business relationship with us
  • Support Communications: Retained for a reasonable period to provide ongoing support
  • Legal Compliance: Some information may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations
  • Ralph Application Data: You control all retention policies for data within your Ralph deployment

International Data Transfers

If we transfer your personal information outside your country of residence, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • Appropriate contractual safeguards and security measures
  • Compliance with applicable data protection laws
  • Industry-standard encryption and security protocols

Remember: With Ralph's on-premise deployment model, your business data never crosses international boundaries unless you configure it to do so.
