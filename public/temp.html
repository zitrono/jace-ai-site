Style Recommendations

Most Professional & Credible:

  • • Style 1: WSJ Editorial (newspaper authority)
  • • Style 6: Financial Times Pullquote (executive focus)
  • • Style 7: Reference Site Inspired (clean, modern)

Most Visually Distinctive:

  • • Style 2: Modern Card with Floating Quote
  • • Style 4: Magazine Feature Style
  • • Style 6: Financial Times Pullquote

Top Recommendation:

Style 6 (Financial Times Pullquote) combines WSJ's editorial credibility with modern visual appeal. The large quote format with executive initials adds gravitas while the accent border creates visual hierarchy that draws attention without being flashy.
